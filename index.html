Welcome To Social Committee Of VSICS

Encourages Socialization, Collaboration and Other Aspects of Teamwork. Being on a workplace social committee is a lot like working with a team on a big project. You come together, brainstorm, collaborate, and create something special.

Dr. Virendra Swarup Educational Foundation, Uttar Pradesh.(VSEF) was established in the year 1989 in memory of Late Dr. Virendra Swarup, MLC & Chairman of Uttar Pradesh Legislative Council and an eminent educationist. Dr. Virendra Swarup Institute of Computer Studies is a part of VSEF. The foundation is also running 10+2 schools in Kanpur and other cities of Uttar Pradesh.

VSEF is a sister concern of Dayanand Siksha Sansthan, Uttar Pradesh., which is managing more than 25 post graduate and intermediate colleges all over Uttar Pradesh & Uttarakhand. VSICS is the first self-financed institute in Kanpur to impart computer education in the form of MCA.

The institute has achieved the distinction of being ISO 9001:2000 certified for providing quality education by maintaining quality services and processes. VSICS in a span of more than 23 years has gained immense recognition as a center of excellence. Its MCA, MBA, BCA & BBA programmes has been designed to create the best talent in the IT & Management field.

About Social Committee

PURPOSE :
The social committee aims to provide a healthy and collegial environment by contributing to the society. We do this by empowering people to manage their own lives; promoting people's independence; supporting people's social inclusion and participation in society; and helping people to keep safe and well.

VISION :
To foster and transform develop our society by building conscious and capable citizens through the provision of comprehensive, integrated and sustainable social development services.

AGENDA :
Organise a set of events to encourage and ensure meaningful contribution of the student club of VSICS to society fostering a healthy community identity.

MISSION:
To INITIATE, EMPOWER and SUSTAIN non- violent, collaborative, and integral development processes for vulnerable individuals and groups to build a better future.

OBJECTIVE:
The primary objective of social action is to bring about solutions to
••mass problems
••establish equality
••improve mass conditions
••redistribute power and resources(human, material and moral)

GOALS :
The goals of social committee are:-
••Social welfare.
••develops harmony.
••Dignity and worth of the person.
••Importance of human relationships.
••Integrity.
••Competence.
